About

Hello, I am an associate professor of computer science at KAIST. I got my PhD in 2008 from MIT and joined KAIST in the same year. My research interests are in developing and applying machine learning models for natural language processing. In our research group, we look at various data such as news, social media, Wikipedia, and programming education.
