About

Hello, I am an associate professor in computer science at KAIST. I got my PhD in 2008 from MIT and I joined KAIST in the same year. My research interests are in developing and applying machine learning models for human social behavior data. In our research group, we look at a variety of data such as social media (e.g., Twitter), collaborative editing (e.g., Wikipedia), driving behavior, and programming education.
